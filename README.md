# Shopify Analytics Pipeline — E-Commerce Market

End-to-end analytics pipeline built for a Shopify-based fitness equipment store operating in the DACH market (Germany, Austria, Switzerland).

## What This Project Does
- **Cohort Retention Analysis** — tracks customer retention month over month
- **Channel Performance** — compares Meta Ads, Google Ads, Direct, and Email by orders and AOV
- **AI-Powered Ticket Analysis** — categorizes support tickets and detects sentiment
- **Regional Sales Distribution** — breaks down performance across DACH countries

## Tech Stack
Python, Pandas, SQL (SQLite), Matplotlib, Seaborn

## Key Findings
- Meta Ads drives the most volume (398 orders)
- Email has the highest AOV (€313) — best quality customers
- Shipping & Delivery is the #1 support issue (34% of tickets)
- January 2025 cohort shows ~70% Month 1 retention
